Who are we

We are Trashers. A completely communitte driven initative with the goal of cleaning up the city

What we do

We work together with the communitte to locate and pick up trash. This both keeps our city clean and gives people jobs

How it Works

Users can report trash on the site. Any one can go pick up this trash and mark off the location. Once you pick up the trash take it to the city council to get some money. The more trash you bring in the more money you get.
